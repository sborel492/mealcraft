What is MealCraft?

"What do you want for dinner?" is a dreaded phrase in many households. Making that decision every single day is not an appealing thought. But with MealCraft, you can have some of the work taken out of dinner by letting our tools make the decisions for you! Unlike other menu generators that can have time-consuming recipes or only have recipes for a specific diet, MealCraft recipes are realistic and don't require you to be on a specific diet to use our tools. Life is full of anxiety, let MealCraft take a little bit away.

What can you do in MealCraft?

We have many recipes available to browse, with more being added regularly. You can search our recipes by name or category, and you can build a weekly menu yourself or have one generated for you.
